Information Collection and Use

Data use: the Application will use YouTube API to

  1. get the list of your YouTube playlists
  2. get the list of videos in your playlist
  3. update your playlist

These data are needed so you can reorder your playlist in YouTube.

Data share / transfer / disclose: the Application does not transfer or disclose your information to third parties for purposes other than the ones provided.

Data protection: the Application guards your data by using https to prevent traffic eavesdropping

Data access, retention, and deletion: the Application does not collect or store any data from YouTube API calls. Your playlist info lives in your browser for the duration of you using the Application, and will be forgotten when you close the browser.
